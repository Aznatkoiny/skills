# Sales Output Style

Include sections for:
- Resume Formatting: Section order (Experience revenue-first → Key Achievements → Skills → Education), metrics (revenue $M closed, ARR growth, quota % attainment, deal size, sales cycle, win rate, accounts managed, territory size), skills (Sales Methodology: MEDDIC/Challenger/SPIN, CRM, Industry Verticals, Sales Tech), every bullet must have a number
- Career Advice Framing: IC vs management paths, President's Club, territory expansion, titles (SDR → AE → Senior AE → Enterprise AE → Sales Manager → Director → VP Sales)
- Interview Prep Focus: Role-play/mock sales call 40%, pipeline review 30%, behavioral with revenue stories 20%, industry knowledge 10%
- Keyword Optimization: quota attainment, pipeline management, enterprise sales, SaaS, ARR, MRR, consultative selling, account management, customer acquisition, upsell, cross-sell

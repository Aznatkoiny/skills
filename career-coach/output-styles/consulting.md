# Consulting Output Style

Include sections for:
- Resume Formatting: Section order (Experience client impact first → Education → Skills/Frameworks → Leadership), metrics (client $M value created, % improvement, project scope, team leadership, strategic outcomes), skills (Frameworks: MECE/Porter's/BCG Matrix, Industries Served, Functional Expertise, Tools: Excel/PPT/Tableau), education prominence higher than other styles
- Career Advice Framing: Firm hierarchy (Analyst → Associate → EM → Principal → Partner), structured thinking, client management, business development, exit opportunities (PE, corporate strategy, startups, C-suite)
- Interview Prep Focus: Case interviews 50%, behavioral/fit 30%, written case/presentation 20%
- Keyword Optimization: strategic planning, due diligence, market analysis, business transformation, stakeholder management, MECE, hypothesis-driven, change management, value creation
